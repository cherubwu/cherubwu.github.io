<div class="poem-wrap">

<div class="poem-title-block">
  <h1>🌹 悬崖玫瑰 · The Cliff Rose</h1>
  <div class="subtitle">Cherainboow &nbsp;·&nbsp; June 2026</div>
</div>

<div class="poem-section">
  <div class="poem-col">
    <div class="poem-section-title">一 · 风的捉迷藏</div>
    <div class="poem-text">
      <p>我开在云朵停靠的悬崖 ☁️<br>
      把刺藏在红色的裙摆底下 💃</p>

      <p>我长得那么高<br>
      不是为了让人仰望 ⛰️<br>
      只是想在风吹过来的时候<br>
      第一个 和它撞个满怀 🍃</p>

      <p>如果你想摘我，可要轻轻地<br>
      我一害怕 🥺<br>
      就会变成一滴雨 漏进你的手心里 💧</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="poem-col">
    <div class="poem-section-title">I · Hide and Seek with the Wind</div>
    <div class="poem-text">
      <p>I bloom on the cliff where the clouds come to rest ☁️<br>
      Hiding my thorns beneath a red flowing dress 💃</p>

      <p>I grow so high up<br>
      Not to make people look up at me ⛰️<br>
      But just so when the wind blows by<br>
      I can be the first to collide into its embrace 🍃</p>

      <p>If you wish to pick me, you must be very gentle<br>
      If I get scared 🥺<br>
      I will turn into a raindrop and slip right through your hands 💧</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="poem-section">
  <div class="poem-col">
    <div class="poem-section-title">二 · 清晨的悬念</div>
    <div class="poem-text">
      <p>悬崖上的玫瑰<br>
      今早偷喝了一口雾气 🌫️</p>

      <p>它红得像一个 刚刚说漏嘴的秘密 🤫<br>
      山脚下的人都在猜<br>
      它到底是为了谁 摇晃了一整天 🤷</p>

      <p>它偏不说话 🤐<br>
      只把尖尖的刺，指向最亮的那颗星星 ⭐</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="poem-col">
    <div class="poem-section-title">II · The Mystery of Morning</div>
    <div class="poem-text">
      <p>The rose on the cliff<br>
      Stole a sip of the morning mist today 🌫️</p>

      <p>It blushed as red as a secret just accidentally spilled 🤫<br>
      The people at the foot of the mountain are all guessing<br>
      For whom did it sway all day long 🤷</p>

      <p>But it refuses to say a word 🤐<br>
      Simply pointing its sharp thorns at the brightest star ⭐</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="poem-section">
  <div class="poem-col">
    <div class="poem-section-title">三 · 绝对的雀跃</div>
    <div class="poem-text">
      <p>爱是一只长满刺的小狐狸 🦊<br>
      偏偏跑去最陡峭的岩石上跳舞 🩰</p>

      <p>它每一次转圈<br>
      都让看的人 捏一把汗 💦<br>
      可它自己高兴极了 🥰</p>

      <p>它说：你看，只有站得这么高<br>
      月亮才会把最干净的光 🌙<br>
      只借给我一个人 👑</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="poem-col">
    <div class="poem-section-title">III · Absolute Joy</div>
    <div class="poem-text">
      <p>Love is a little fox covered in thorns 🦊<br>
      That insists on dancing on the steepest rock 🩰</p>

      <p>With every spin it takes<br>
      It makes the watchers hold their breath in sweat 💦<br>
      Yet it is absolutely thrilled itself 🥰</p>

      <p>It says: Look, only by standing this high<br>
      Will the moon lend its purest light 🌙<br>
      Solely and only to me 👑</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

</div>
