# ✨ *Scottish Fantazy*

## 🌧️ **Day 2 --- Cerulean Rain & Cyberpunk Greenwich**

> *"We chased the sunrise and found rainbows in the drizzle."*

We woke **early to chase the sunrise**, jogging through still-sleepy
**Cambridge** streets as the sky blazed **pink, orange, and fire-red**.\
He was buzzing with energy; I was already questioning all my life
choices and realizing I desperately needed to restart my exercise
routine.

The sky burned brighter as we ran --- a painter gone mad with color ---
and by the time we stumbled back, I was ready for a long shower. We left
a little late, smelling of shampoo and victory.

Then, *London time.*\
We met **Charlie's best friend and our classmate** in the City --- a
trio armed with coffee and a questionable sense of direction. From
Liverpool Street to the Gherkin, along the river Thames past Big Ben,
the London Eye, Tate Modern, and St Paul's --- we walked the city's
heartbeat.\
We wandered **Covent Garden** and **Soho**, neon signs winking like
mischievous spirits.\
Lunch at **Japes Pizza** became an endless laughter reel: too much
cheese, too many stories, exactly enough happiness.

Then the sky began its art project again. The Queen's walk shimmered;
**the Thames turned cerulean**, reflective and mysterious --- like a
melancholy AI poet from a future London. We took the Uber ferry down the
Thames to Greenwich, the fog rolling in to swallow towers, ships, and
maybe a few timelines.

> We called it *"The Cyberpunk Hour of Rain."*\
> Somewhere between raindrops and reality, we lost track of time ---
> maybe that *was* the Greenwich magic. ⏰💙
