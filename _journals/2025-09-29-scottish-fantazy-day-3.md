# ✨ *Scottish Fantazy*

## 🌦️ **Day 3 --- The Wrong Train to the Right Day**

> *"Sometimes the wrong train takes you to the right sunset."*

A **massive breakfast**, a confident smile... and the *wrong train.*
Classic us.\
But the journey turned cinematic: the **sea widened** as we crossed from
England to Scotland, **Newcastle's gothic spires** waving us onward
through mist and legend.

And then --- *woooha!* --- **Edinburgh arrived** faster than our hearts
could.

We dropped our bags at a downtown apartment --- No.1 Apartments George
IV Bridge , right above the famous Elephant House --- storyland
downstairs, our little nest upstairs. Before dinner, we climbed to
**Calton Hill** where the drizzle turned gold. We took tons of photos
--- wind in our hair, city at our feet, the kind of pictures you keep
for rainy mornings and anniversaries.

First adventure as a domestic duo: grocery shopping together ---
treasure-hunting at the 美麒麟 Asian market, then I snagged fresh veg
from Sainsbury's near the university. He headed back to cook (chef mode:
on), and I followed with clinking bags and giggles in the cobbled dusk.
Back home, our **homemade hotpot** bubbled like a love potion --- spicy,
steamy, gloriously chaotic.

Outside, Edinburgh whispered; inside, everything felt warm and right.

> "One Day," we said --- but it already felt like forever. 🫶🌇🔥
