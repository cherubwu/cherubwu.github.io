# ✨ *Scottish Fantazy*

## 🏞️ **Day 4 --- From Arthur's Seat to the Sea (and the Toilet Tragedy)**

> *"Sunshine, squirrels, and an inconvenient plot twist."*

A morning jog, a **classic brunch**, and then the noble climb of
**Arthur's Seat**.\
The wind kissed us; the sunshine applauded. "Hi, Sunshine!" we waved
like fools.

We strolled through **galleries**, **Edinburgh Castle**, and the
**National Museum**, where I bowed respectfully to **Dolly the sheep**
--- a national icon in wool.

In **Princes Street Park**, we met a curious squirrel... and the tragedy
of a *closed toilet.*\
My dignity suffered. My throat too.

But evening turned the story around.\
We wandered hand-in-hand through the lamplight, our **romantic night
walk** humming with laughter and gentle quiet.

At **Portobello Beach**, the sky deepened into **blue hour** --- not a
five-star beach, maybe three, but perfectly ours.\
Then: **hot bath. steam. soft laughter.**\
The day ended like a kiss under the moon. 🫧💙🌊
