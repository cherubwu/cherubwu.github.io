# ✨ *Scottish Fantazy*

## 🌬️ **Day 5 --- Dundee Dramas & Perth Fantasies**

> *"Every misstep a new melody."*

We **missed the train to Dundee** --- destiny's way of keeping things
interesting.\
**Costa coffee** saved us, and soon we were playing **poker on
ScotRail**, cards and laughter between raindrops.

**Dundee** greeted us with rain, art, and wind sharp enough to etch
memories.\
We biked the city and seaside, chasing pockets of blue sky and salt.

By the time we reached **Perth**, the world exhaled.\
Our **townhouse hotel** was a dream --- garden whispers, tartan grace,
and a breakfast lounge soaked in morning gold.

We crossed the **bridge over the River Tay** into **Kinnoull Hill
Woodland Park**.\
Charlie, my beloved trickster, swore, *"It's not high."* Lies.\
We followed a **schoolgirl-turned-forest-spirit**, her laughter echoing
through moss and mist, and somehow --- up the wrong path --- we found
the right view.\
**Perth spread below like watercolor.**

Dinner at **Cardo** arrived early, as did we.\
**Soup, rosé, steak**, and finally --- *the divine ending:* **chocolate
marquise with pear and lemon sorbet.**

> A night of flavor, warmth, and stolen glances.\
> Maybe the stars above Perth saw it too --- our little fantasy, sealed
> with our first night's kiss. 💋✨
