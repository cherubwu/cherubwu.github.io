# ✨ *Scottish Fantazy*

## 🌞 **Day 1 --- Cambridge & the Beef Bourguignon Redemption**

> *"A day that began with nerves, and ended in laughter and stew."*

I woke up at **5 a.m. without an alarm**, heart fluttering like a fiddle
tune.\
Charlie was arriving today --- somewhere between *sky and planet* ---
and I was a swirl of nerves, hope, and caffeine.

The **sunrise from Cambridge to Heathrow** was a watercolor of honey and
lavender, as if Scotland herself whispered,\
\> *"Aye, make it a good one."*

When we finally met, the world clicked into focus.\
Brunch at the **top-rated Half Cup café near King's Cross** felt like a
dream --- **Oreo pancakes**, **fried-chicken waffles**, and
**smoothies** shared between laughs.

Later, the *London sun*, in rare generosity, smiled down upon us.\
By evening, Cambridge called us home, where our culinary ambition
simmered in a pot: *Beef Bourguignon à la Chaos*.

It was perfect --- until we realised we were **locked out**.\
Standing outside, holding a pot of French glory, we looked at each other
like tragic poets in a domestic comedy. Love is... waiting for me to
fetch the spare key while your dinner loses its steam. 🥹🥂
